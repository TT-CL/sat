SAT - Segmentation and Alignment Tool

Welcome to the Segmentation and Alignment Tool - SAT.
With SAT you can segment documents into Idea Units and connect them to create a set of aligned pairs.
GDPR: This website utilises cookies to store your working data.
Please choose your favored usage mode.

Online Mode

Online mode stores your progress on an online database for indexing and research analysis.
By using this toolkit online you consent to share your data with the research team.
Note: online mode requires a Google account for authentication.

Offline Mode

Offline mode allows you to use the toolkit locally.
No data will be shared with the research team.
Note: ensure you always save your progress to avoid data loss
